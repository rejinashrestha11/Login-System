About Us

WELCOME to our website!!
Our team has been tasked with developing a computer programme that showcases appropriate design of security systems and the application of programming principles to cybersecurity applications. Specifically, we have focused on creating a prototype system that emphasizes the importance of secure user registration processes.

Participating in online communities often requires users to register on the platform by creating a user account. However, this process can be vulnerable to security threats if not implemented properly. Therefore, our prototype system prioritizes secure user registration processes to protect user information.

Our computer programme includes a user interface that prompts the user to create an account by providing a username and password. We have algorithmically determined the strength of the chosen password by the user and provide suitable feedback to the user about the password strength. To accomplish this task, we have researched password strength criteria and incorporated our findings into the programme.

Additionally, we have implemented a captcha function to determine that the registration request is made by a human user rather than a machine, such as a bot. We have researched different types of captchas and implemented one type that best meets the needs of our prototype system.

Throughout the development of our prototype and prototype design, we have embedded good secure system design and applied appropriate cybersecurity principles and techniques. We have made use of robust policies and procedures for password strength, including frequency of change, strength of password, preventing repetition of passwords, and use of encryption.

Overall, our computer programme serves as a strong example of secure system design and cybersecurity principles in action, and we are excited to share our work with you.
